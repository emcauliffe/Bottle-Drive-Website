Bottles Against COVID-19

Thank you!

Bottles Against Covid was an initiative started in late April 2020 to raise money for the fight against COVID-19. People could sign up to get their empty bottles picked up, or start a bottle drive of their own. All bottle collections were completely contactless — those donating bottles simply left their boxes of empties on their front step.

Thanks to the generosity of the community, we raised more than $3000 for St. Joe's Hospital in Toronto.

This site is now simply left up for reference. Code for the original website is available on GitHub.
